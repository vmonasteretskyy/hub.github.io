Automation Automation

Automation is the process of repeating a task multiple times with accuracy. It helps increase productivity of a manual task instantly without spending money to develop a custom application. It is especially important in healthcare since it increases reliability of the task by reducing numbers of errors.

According to studies, Healthcare has about 30-40% automation potential.

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is an emerging technology that can streamline workflow and repetitive processes across organizations. We believe Robotic Process Automation tools like UI Path can be used to build models that can do your everyday work with scale and efficiency than most humans.
